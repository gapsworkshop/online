GAPS online continues the GAPS 2025 workshop, as a recurring online seminar. It provides a regular platform for researchers interested in provable security for symmetric-key cryptography. Topics include proof techniques, security notions, and generic attacks; related topics such as cryptanalysis and mathematical foundations are also welcome.
